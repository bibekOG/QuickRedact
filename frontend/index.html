How to Use Quick Redact

Master the art of screenshot masking in 3 simple steps.

Import Your Image

Drag and drop your file, click the upload button, or simply press Ctrl+V to paste a screenshot directly from your clipboard.

Select & Redact

Click and drag over sensitive areas. You can move, resize, and adjust the intensity of the blur until it's perfect.

Export Instantly

Copy the redacted image back to your clipboard or download it as a high-quality PNG. Your data never leaves your device.

The Privacy-First Utility

Quick Redact was built with one goal in mind: to make data protection accessible and effortless for everyone.

In an age where sharing screenshots is second nature, we often accidentally leak passwords, bank details, or private conversations. Traditional editing tools are either too slow or require uploading your sensitive files to a cloud server.

Quick Redact is different. All processing happens locally in your browser. We never see your images, and we never store your data. It's masking as it should be: professional, precise, and private.

Integrate secure, GDPR-compliant local-equivalent masking directly into your CI/CD or server pipelines.

  -H "Authorization: Bearer qr_live_..." \
  -F "image=@screenshot.png" \
  -F "mode=pixelate" \
  -F "targets=credit_cards,names,phones"
